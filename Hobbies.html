Basketball

One of the hobbies I've had since I was 6-7 years old was playing basketball. I started training basketball at that age and always enjoyed playing it especially with my friends. I was never a team player really. I would win alone against 3 of my friends that were the same age as me and played as much as I did. I miss basketball I haven't played it in the last year as much.
